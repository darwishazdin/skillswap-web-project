About SkillSwap

Learn more about our mission and how we're building a community of learners

Our Mission

SkillSwap was created to break down financial barriers to learning and create meaningful connections within our community. We believe that everyone has valuable skills to share and that learning should be accessible to all.

How It Started

This project began as a web development course project at The American University of Kurdistan. Our team, Culers, recognized the need for a platform where students and community members could exchange knowledge without financial constraints.

What We Stand For

  • Accessibility: Learning opportunities for everyone, regardless of financial situation
  • Community: Building connections and trust through shared learning
  • Empowerment: Helping people recognize and share their unique talents
  • Collaboration: Creating win-win situations through skill exchanges
